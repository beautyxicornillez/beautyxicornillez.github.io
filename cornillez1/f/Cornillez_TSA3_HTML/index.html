About Formula 1

Formula 1 is the highest class of international motorsport, featuring the fastest cars, world-famous drivers, and races hosted around the globe. It is governed by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA) and consists of a series of races known as Grands Prix, held on both purpose-built circuits and public roads. The sport is not only a test of speed, but also of strategy, engineering innovation, and driver skill. Teams invest millions to gain even the smallest competitive edge. Learn more about the sport at Formula1.com.

Top Drivers

Lewis Hamilton

7-time World Champion known for his consistency and dominance with Mercedes. Hamilton holds records for most pole positions and is tied for the most championship titles with Michael Schumacher. Off the track, he is an advocate for diversity in motorsport and environmental issues.

Max Verstappen

Young and fearless, Verstappen has dominated recent seasons with Red Bull Racing. Known for his aggressive yet calculated driving style, he won his first World Championship in 2021 and followed it up with multiple consecutive titles, establishing himself as one of the sport’s modern greats.

Charles Leclerc

Ferrari’s star driver, admired for his speed and determination on the track. Leclerc joined Ferrari in 2019 and quickly became a fan favorite due to his raw talent and strong qualifying performances. Despite struggles with team strategy, he continues to shine with remarkable racecraft.
